Help

This is a application to training listening and writing words. The app selects a word, pronounces it and expects you to enter it correctly. You can set up how long and difficult the words will be offered. Push Space to listen word again. Push Enter to skip current word.

The words where errors are made will be shown in next session. Also words with errors will be added to bookmarks automatically. You can add a word to bookmarks by click on word boxes.

The score indicates your progress to persuade you to beat your previous record or type a daily bunch of words.
The scores sharing between users is under question.

If app don't work properly, e.g. you don't listen any sound or you see permanent "Loading..." instead "Play"-button on first screen, it's means that your browser is obsolete. Use Chrome to avoid troubles.
Minimal supported Android version is 4.1 with Chrome 69.

If you have any questions, comments or suggestions, just let me know lb.im@yandex.ru or visit project page.
